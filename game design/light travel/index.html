The One-Sheet

Light Travel

VR/PC
Target Age: 13-up
Intended ESRB Rating: E

Game Summary

In the future era, you are on a spaceship that travels in a galaxy. The galaxy has diverse planets as your departure and destination. Using the advanced technology, the spaceship can travel at the speed of light. Enjoy your extraordinary travel from one planet to another and explore the spectacular landscape of diverse planets!

Game Outline

The player can choose the departure planet and the destination planet. The travel time depends on the distance and the speed. In this galaxy, the distance between planets is enormous. To reduce the travel time, when the player departs from a planet, he or she should speed up. To land safely, when the player arrives at a planet, he or she should slow down. The speeding up process and the slowing down process require the player to press the speeding up key and slowing down key respectively. Due to the design of the spaceship, the frequency of speeding up should be slower and slower. Due to the safety issue, the frequency of slowing down should also be slower and slower. During the travel, the player can see the distance from the departure and the destination, and the travel speed. According to the physics theory, in light travel, the time flow is much slower than usual. Therefore, the landscape of the planet may have changed a lot when the player arrives.

USP (Unique Selling Points)

  • Dynamic sight depending on the location and the speed, and multiple perspectives of the spaceship
  • Ethereal background music composed by famous composers
  • Vivid simulation of high-speed travel based on the strict physics theory
  • Unusual time flow and various stages of the planet
  • Meditation during the travel, meditation on diverse planets, and soothing way of interaction
